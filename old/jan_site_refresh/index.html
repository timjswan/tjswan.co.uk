At last! A website to help you learn more about your reading preferences and help you think about reading and well-being.

We believe that reading is creative, good for you and fun! We are currently involved in a new project called Get Into Reading East Lancashire. This project will introduce a new type of reading group where the emphasis is on being read to and just relaxing. To find out more about this project please contact us, or come to a group! The organisations that are linked to this project are The Reader Organisation, Lancashire County Library and Information Service and NHS East Lancashire. The current list of groups is here.

Find out more about us in our background information page.
