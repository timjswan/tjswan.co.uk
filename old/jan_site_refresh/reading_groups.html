Get Into Reading

We have just launched several new reading groups across East Lancashire which will help you to relax while we read to you. Get Into Reading groups aim to improve mental well-being through the shared reading of good books. The groups run in libraries and other settings and all are free, with each group led by a facilitator who reads stories and poems aloud. All reading takes place within the session (thus reducing stress on you if you do not have time to read or if you struggle to read alone for whatever reason). The group often stops to discuss people's immediate thoughts and responses to what has just been read, but people are under no pressure to participate more than they want or are able to. Groups may include carers, people with mental or physical health issues, people who are recently bereaved or in need of support.
